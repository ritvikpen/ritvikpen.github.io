Projects

Parkinson's Algorithm

For the Alameda County Science Fair, my team and I developed an algorithm in the programming language R. It would analyze the hand tremors displayed by Parkinson's patients and quantitatively determine whether their condition was improving or worsening. It won the first place prize at the Science Fair that year.

Tap Test Application

Tap Test is an Android Application created by me for a Stanford MedX research group. It assists their research by collecting data about Alzheimer's patients' reflexes. It measures the amount of "taps" the patient can perform onto the phone in multiple trials, carries out basic data analysis of this information, and then sends the data via email to the research team.
